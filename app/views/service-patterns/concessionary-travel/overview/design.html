Journey map

This diagram shows the pages the user should go through in the online service and how they link together. It's based on extensive user research and service design best practice.

How to use the map

Controls

Hover over the diagram to show the controls. Use the controls to optimise the map and zoom in and out. The controls show at the bottom of the page when the map is optimised.

Clicking on a section name will take you to the relevant section of the pattern guidance.

Prototype links

Clicking on an individual page in the diagram will take you to the relevant prototype page.
